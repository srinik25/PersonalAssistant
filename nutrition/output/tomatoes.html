📜 History & Origin

Tomatoes originated in western South America and were first domesticated in Mexico. They were introduced to Europe in the 16th century and have since become a staple in cuisines worldwide.

🍳 Recipe: Tomato Basil Bruschetta

A simple, fresh appetizer featuring ripe tomatoes and fragrant basil on toasted bread.

⏲ Prep 10 minutes
🔥 Cook 5 minutes
🍴 Servings 4

Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices of whole-grain baguette

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, basil, garlic, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast in the oven for 5 minutes or until golden brown.
  4. Top each slice with the tomato mixture and serve immediately.
