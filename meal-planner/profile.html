Important: Please do not enter any PII or PHI in this form. What is PII/PHI?
PII (Personally Identifiable Information) includes full legal name, Social Security number, date of birth, home address, phone number, driver's license number, financial account numbers, or any data that could uniquely identify you.

PHI (Protected Health Information) includes medical diagnoses, prescription details, lab results, health insurance IDs, treatment history, or any information about your health conditions that is linked to your identity.

First name or nickname, general age, and broad dietary preferences are fine. Avoid entering anything that could be used to identify you or your medical history.
