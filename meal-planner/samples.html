Weekly Summary

Avg Calories: ~1,930/day  |  Avg Protein: ~69g/day  |  Avg Fiber: ~28g/day  |  Dining Out: 3 meals/week (Tue lunch, Thu dinner, Sat lunch)

Strengths: High fiber from dal, chapati, and legumes. Good variety of protein sources (eggs, paneer, chicken, salmon, tofu). Daily fruits and Greek yogurt. Balanced Indian + American fusion. Healthy fats from avocado, nuts, and homemade pesto.

Suggested Improvements: Add more leafy greens and raw vegetables. Increase water intake with high-fiber meals. Swap one rice meal for quinoa or millets for variety. Track dining out portions to stay within calorie targets.
